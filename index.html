The path every idea takes

Idea, build, test, grow.

Every project on the board moves through the same five stages. It's not a form to fill in once — it's a path you actually walk.

01

Idea

Write down the thought, however rough. This is the starting line, not a pitch.

02

Explore

Look into the problem. Who has it? Has someone tried this before?

03

Build

Make the first version — a sketch, a prototype, a working draft.

04

Test

Put it in front of real people. See what breaks, what lands.

05

Showcase

Show what you built. Whatever the outcome, it's now something real.

An idea is only the beginning.

The Incubation Hub isn't a place to submit an idea and wait. It's a direction — a way to help student ideas become visible, structured, and buildable, one stage at a time.

No idea here is judged as a finished startup. A research question, a class experiment, and an early product all belong on the same board, at the same stage: the beginning.

Future roadmap: broader mentorship, showcase events and institutional support are directions the Hub may grow toward, subject to review by Hillwoods. Nothing here represents funding, guaranteed mentorship, or a promise of outcome.
